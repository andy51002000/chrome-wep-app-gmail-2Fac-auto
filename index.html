How it works

1. Request permission

Sign in with Google and grant read-only access to your Gmail inbox using OAuth 2.0.

2. Pull recent emails

The app scans messages from the last five minutes with smart filters to skip noise.

3. Extract codes & links

Structured parsing finds numeric and alphanumeric codes plus actionable verification URLs.

4. Match your domain

Links from the domain you're currently visiting are highlighted for quick confirmation.
